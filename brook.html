Brook

Brook, the musician of the Straw Hat Pirates, is a living skeleton brought back to life by the Revive-Revive Fruit. He has a cheerful demeanor and a love for music, especially the violin. Brook dreams of reuniting with Laboon, a whale he befriended long ago.
